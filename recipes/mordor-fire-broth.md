# 🌋 Mordor Fire Broth

For when the broth must burn as bright as the fires of Mount Doom.

## Ingredients
- 2L beef stock
- 4 tbsp chili bean paste (doubanjiang)
- 3 dried chilies
- 2 tbsp Sichuan peppercorns
- 1 tbsp ash salt (smoked salt)
- Garlic, lots

## Method
1. Bloom the chilies and peppercorns in oil.
2. Stir in the bean paste, then the stock.
3. Simmer 25 min. Approach with caution.
