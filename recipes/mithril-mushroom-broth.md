# 🍄 Mithril Mushroom Broth

Light as mithril, deep as the mines of Moria.

## Ingredients
- 2L mushroom stock
- 200g shiitake
- 200g enoki
- 1 block silken tofu
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 sheet kombu

## Method
1. Steep kombu in cold stock, then heat gently (don't boil).
2. Add mushrooms, simmer 15 min.
3. Slide in the tofu at the end so it stays silky.
