# 🌿 Shire Garden Broth

A gentle, herby broth fit for a lazy afternoon in Hobbiton.

## Ingredients
- 2L vegetable stock
- 3 leeks, sliced
- 2 carrots
- 1 thumb ginger
- A fistful of garden herbs (parsley, thyme)
- Salt to taste

## Method
1. Sweat the leeks and carrots until soft.
2. Add stock and ginger, simmer 20 min.
3. Toss in herbs at the end. Serve with potatoes — boil 'em, mash 'em, stick 'em in a hot pot.
